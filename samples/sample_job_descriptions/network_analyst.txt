Title: Network Analyst

**Job Title:** Network Analyst (Network Performance)
**Company:** Andersons
**Location:** Kinshasa, Democratic Republic Of Congo

### About the Role
Andersons is seeking an experienced and analytical Network Performance Analyst to join our team in Kinshasa. In this critical part-time role, you will be responsible for monitoring, analyzing, and optimizing our network infrastructure to ensure peak performance, reliability, and efficiency. You will play a key role in identifying and resolving complex network issues to support our operational excellence.

### Key Responsibilities
*   Proactively monitor and analyze network performance data to identify trends, anomalies, and potential issues.
*   Diagnose and troubleshoot complex network problems, including identifying performance bottlenecks, latency, and packet loss.
*   Develop and recommend strategies for network optimization, including configuration changes and hardware upgrades.
*   Collaborate closely with network engineering teams to implement performance improvements and validate their effectiveness.
*   Conduct regular capacity planning and performance forecasting to support business growth and evolving network demands.
*   Generate detailed performance reports and dashboards for technical teams and management, providing actionable insights.

### Required Qualifications
*   **Experience:** 5 to 14 years of professional experience in network performance analysis, network engineering, or a related field.
*   **Education:** Master of Technology (M.Tech) or an equivalent advanced degree in a relevant technical discipline.

### Required Skills
*   In-depth knowledge of network performance analysis and monitoring methodologies.
*   Hands-on experience with network monitoring tools (e.g., Wireshark, SolarWinds, Nagios).
*   Strong troubleshooting and problem-solving skills for complex network issues.
*   Expertise in network capacity planning and performance forecasting.
*   Advanced data analysis skills to interpret network metrics, logs, and packet captures.
*   Deep understanding of core network protocols (TCP/IP, BGP, OSPF, DNS, DHCP).

Title: Human Resources Manager

**About the Role**

New Oriental Education & Technology Group is seeking a dynamic and versatile Human Resources Manager to join our team in Baghdad. In this HR Generalist role, you will be a cornerstone of our operations, responsible for managing the full spectrum of HR functions and fostering a positive, productive workplace culture. You will serve as a key strategic partner to both employees and leadership, ensuring our HR practices align with organizational goals and legal standards.

**Key Responsibilities**

*   Manage the end-to-end recruitment and onboarding process to attract, hire, and retain top-tier talent.
*   Develop, implement, and maintain comprehensive HR policies and procedures, ensuring compliance with local labor laws and regulations.
*   Serve as the primary point of contact for employee relations, expertly handling inquiries, concerns, and conflict resolution with professionalism and confidentiality.
*   Administer employee compensation, benefits programs, and performance management systems to support employee growth and retention.
*   Maintain accurate and confidential employee records and manage HR documentation.
*   Champion employee engagement initiatives to cultivate a positive and collaborative work environment.

**Required Qualifications**

*   **Experience:** 0 to 14 years of experience in a Human Resources capacity. We encourage candidates at all levels, from recent graduates to seasoned professionals, to apply.
*   **Education:** A Master of Commerce (M.Com) degree is required.

**Required Skills**

*   In-depth knowledge of core HR policies and procedures.
*   Proven experience in managing employee relations and conflict resolution.
*   Strong background in full-cycle recruitment and talent acquisition.
*   Excellent verbal and written communication and interpersonal skills.

**Compensation & Benefits**

*   **Salary Range:** $59,000 - $90,000 USD per annum, commensurate with experience.
*   **Benefits:** Transportation Benefits; Professional Development Opportunities; Bonuses and Incentive Programs; Profit-Sharing; Employee Discounts

**Location & Work Arrangement**

*   **Location:** City of Baghdad, Iraq
*   **Work Arrangement:** Full-Time, On-site

Title: Graphic Designer

**Job Title:** Graphic Designer (UI/UX Designer)
**Company:** Performance Food Group

### About the Role
Performance Food Group is seeking a creative and detail-oriented UI/UX Designer to join our team on a contract basis. In this role, you will be responsible for creating intuitive, user-friendly, and visually appealing interfaces for our digital products, including websites and applications. You will play a pivotal part in shaping our user experience, ensuring a seamless and engaging journey for our customers from concept to launch.

### Key Responsibilities
*   Design and deliver wireframes, user stories, user journeys, and mockups optimized for a wide range of devices and interfaces.
*   Conduct comprehensive user research and evaluate user feedback to enhance and polish the user experience.
*   Perform usability testing to identify and troubleshoot UX problems, such as responsiveness, navigation, and accessibility issues.
*   Collaborate closely with product managers and developers to implement innovative solutions for the product direction, visuals, and experience.
*   Create and maintain design systems and style guides to ensure brand consistency across all digital platforms.
*   Translate complex concepts into simple, elegant, and user-centric design solutions.

### Required Qualifications
*   **Experience:** 3 to 11 years of professional experience in a UI/UX design role.
*   **Education:** Master of Commerce (M.Com) or a related field.

### Required Skills
*   Proficiency in User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UX) design principles.
*   Expertise with wireframing and prototyping tools (e.g., Sketch, Figma, Adobe XD).
*   Demonstrated experience in conducting usability testing and gathering user feedback.
*   Strong understanding of information architecture and interaction design.
*   In-depth knowledge of mobile-first and responsive design principles.
*   Excellent visual design skills with a keen eye for detail.
*   Strong communication and collaboration skills to work effectively with cross-functional teams.

### Compensation & Benefits
*   **Salary Range:** $61,000 - $126,000 annually, commensurate with experience.
*   **Benefits:** Legal Assistance; Bonuses and Incentive Programs; Wellness Programs; Employee Discounts; Retirement Plans

### Location & Work Arrangement
*   **Location:** Port Moresby, Papua New Guinea
*   **Work Type:** Contract

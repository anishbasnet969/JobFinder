Title: Front-End Engineer

**Job Title:** Front-End Engineer (UI/UX Intern)
**Company:** Bath & Body Works

### About the Role
Bath & Body Works is seeking a creative and detail-oriented Front-End Engineer Intern to join our dynamic team. In this role, you will focus on creating user-friendly and visually appealing interfaces, translating design concepts into functional, responsive web applications that optimize the user experience and interaction.

### Key Responsibilities
* Translate UI/UX design wireframes and mockups into clean, efficient, and responsive code for our web platforms.
* Design and develop user-centric interfaces, ensuring a seamless and intuitive user journey from start to finish.
* Conduct comprehensive usability testing and gather user feedback to iterate on and continuously improve interface designs.
* Collaborate closely with product managers, designers, and other engineers to implement innovative solutions for product direction, visuals, and experience.
* Ensure all front-end development adheres to modern web accessibility standards (WCAG) and best practices.
* Create and maintain interactive prototypes and style guides to ensure consistency across all digital products.

### Required Qualifications
* **Experience:** 1 to 12 years of relevant experience in front-end development or UI/UX design.
* **Education:** Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) or a related field.

### Required Skills
* Proficiency in UI/UX design principles and best practices.
* Strong experience in frontend web development, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
* Demonstrated ability with wireframing and prototyping tools (e.g., Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD).
* Experience with user research and usability testing methodologies.
* In-depth knowledge of web accessibility standards (WCAG).
* Expertise in implementing responsive and adaptive design.
* Excellent collaboration and communication skills.

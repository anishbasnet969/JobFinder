Title: Social Worker

**Job Title:** Social Worker - Mental Health Counselor
**Company:** Nationwide
**Location:** Gibraltar, Gibraltar

### About the Role
Nationwide is seeking a compassionate and skilled Social Worker to join our team as a Mental Health Counselor on a temporary basis. In this vital role, you will provide essential counseling and therapy services, empowering individuals and groups to navigate their mental health challenges and improve their overall well-being.

### Key Responsibilities
*   Conduct comprehensive mental health assessments to accurately diagnose psychological, behavioral, and emotional disorders.
*   Develop, implement, and regularly review individualized, evidence-based treatment plans in collaboration with clients.
*   Provide professional mental health counseling and psychotherapy to individuals and groups using a variety of therapeutic modalities.
*   Offer immediate crisis intervention and support to clients experiencing acute psychological distress.
*   Maintain accurate, timely, and confidential clinical documentation in accordance with ethical standards and legal requirements.
*   Collaborate with other healthcare professionals and community resources to ensure comprehensive and coordinated client care.

### Required Qualifications
*   **Experience:** 3 to 14 years of professional experience in mental health counseling, psychotherapy, or a related social work field.
*   **Education:** Master of Technology (M.Tech) degree.
*   **Licensure:** Must hold a valid and current professional license or certification to practice in the relevant jurisdiction.

### Required Skills
*   Proficiency in a range of counseling techniques and psychotherapy models.
*   Strong competency in mental health assessment and diagnostic formulation.
*   Expertise in clinical treatment planning and progress monitoring.
*   Advanced skills in crisis intervention and de-escalation.
*   In-depth knowledge of and adherence to professional ethical standards.
*   Exceptional empathy, active listening, and interpersonal abilities.
*   Demonstrated cultural competence and sensitivity to diverse populations.
*   Meticulous documentation and record-keeping skills.
*   Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
*   Commitment to professional self-care practices.
